This service allows merchants create and manage their customers easily.

## Conclusion

This Customer Service provides a robust framework for organizations to manage customer relationships from creation to account setup and verification, with the added benefit of comprehensive monitoring through the Edge dashboard. By leveraging this service, organizations can ensure a smooth, compliant, and efficient customer management process, enhancing their overall operational effectiveness and gaining valuable insights into customer activities.
