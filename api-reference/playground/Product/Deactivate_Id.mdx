---
title: "Deactivate Product by its ID"
api: "PATCH /products/deactivate/{productId}"
description: "The endpoint allows you to deactivate a product by its ID."
---

### **Path Parameter**

- `productId`: The ID of the product to deactivate (e.g., `1404e81a-0bf1-11f0-a8a9-6045bd97b81d`).
